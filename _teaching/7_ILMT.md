## 📌 About the Lecture

A public lecture titled **"Observing the Wonders of Nature: From Camera Obscura to the 4-m International Liquid Mirror Telescope (ILMT)"** will explore the evolution of observational astronomy—from early optical devices to modern cutting-edge facilities like the ILMT.

The talk will highlight how innovative telescope technologies are transforming time-domain astronomy and enabling new discoveries.

---

## 🎤 Speaker

**Prof. Jean Surdej**  
Senior Professor, University of Liège, Belgium  

Prof. Jean Surdej is an internationally renowned astrophysicist and Honorary Full Professor and Research Director at the University of Liège. He has served as an ESA astronomer at NASA and held leadership roles at the European Southern Observatory.  

He is currently the Principal Investigator of India’s **4-m International Liquid Mirror Telescope (ILMT)**, inaugurated in 2023, and has led over 100 observing programs on the Hubble Space Telescope and other major facilities.
